          Game Projects

          Colony Defender

          Colony Defender is a simple 1-bit shoot-em-up in the style of Atari 2600 games. As game engine I used Godot v3.4.2. and the sprites I drew with Aseprite. It was the first game I finished on my own, without following any tutorials.

          For me this was a very important step in game design. I stepped back from big, unrealistic ideas, and worked on something I was certain I could build and finish. I learned how to research programming questions and overcome problems by restructuring them into small, manageable steps.
